About Global Mosquito Alert

Global Mosquito Alert is a community of scientists, policymakers, vector control specialists, educators, and public volunteers interested in leveraging citizen science to track and combat mosquito-borne disease. Participating projects contribute resources to a platform and secretariat supporting (1) a toolkit of good practices for working with diverse stakeholders; (2) standardized protocols and processes for data collection and validation; (3) a directory of participating projects; and, (4) a directory of individual experts.
